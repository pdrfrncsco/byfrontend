Club Legacy

Founded in 1980 by the Angolan oil company Sonangol, Petro de Luanda quickly rose to become the most successful football institution in Angola. With a heritage built on technical excellence and community roots, the club has secured over 15 Girabola titles, establishing a dominance that transcends domestic borders.

Their journey in African football is marked by legendary continental runs, representing Luanda with pride and developing some of the continent's finest talents through their prestigious youth academy.
